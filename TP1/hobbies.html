Tottenham Hotspur

Tottenham Hotspur is an English professional football club in Tottenham, London, that competes in the Premier League.

Tottenham Hotspur Stadium has been the club's home ground since April 2019, replacing their former home of White Hart Lane, which had been demolished to make way for the new stadium on the same site. Their training ground is on Hotspur Way in Bulls Cross in the London Borough of Enfield.
